Mini Grooms

Ideal for coat care in-between your regular full groom. A mini groom consists of a bath, dry and brushout. Foot trim, hygiene trim and an eye trim.

Bath & Dry Only

Massaging hydrobath using deshedding shampoo and a full groom out to remove excess and dead hair. (built up hair will incur an additional charge)

Full Grooms

A full groom consists of Health & Skin Check, Hygiene Trim, Massaging hydrobath using premium quality shampoo and conditioner, plus clipping and scissoring to breed standard or your preference.

Hand Stripping

Available for most terrier breeds on Request, this is the process of removing the dead top coat by hand by plucking, or using a stripping knife.
